About

Have you thought about gifts for out of town guests in their hotel rooms? What about ambient lighting in your reception to set the mood? There are a thousand things to think of when planning a wedding, but also a budget to stick to. We take those worrisome details off your shoulders so the two of you can enjoy yourselves, your party, and the company of your friends and family during this crazy time.

We offer a variety of packages at Without a Hitch and work out a unique plan for every couple. Our services range from vendor coordination to design and creative input. Leave the stress to us with our wedding day event direction services.

Let us leave you with happy planning memories and a fun wedding day by taking your stress away.

Relax. It's your wedding.

  • Living in Tucson for 17 years, Without A Hitch owner and lead planner Desiree Groseclose knows the ins and outs of the city and its vendors. Desiree has years of experience working with brides through weddings, photography, and retail. She believes every wedding should reflect a couple's personal style.
